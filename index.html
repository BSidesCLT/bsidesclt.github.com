Welcome

BSides Charlotte is an annual gathering of information security enthusiasts from all walks of life. It's a place where professionals, students and hobbyists all come together to teach and learn.

This year it will be taking place May 6th and 7th and we are excited to be hosting the event at UNC Charlotte Center City. Further details can be found below.
