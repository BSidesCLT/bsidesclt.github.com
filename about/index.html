History

Security BSides is an idea and a information security un-conference that started back in 2009 in Las Vegas.

Security BSides Charlotte (BSidesCLT) was an idea that started with this tweet from @can0beans back in 2012. With help primarily from, but not limited to, @amolesa, @c0ncealed, @_fmm, @damonbrinkley, @th3mojo BSidesCLT was born. @BSidesCLT partnered with the excellent Southeast Linux Fest from 2013 - 2015. 2016 marks the first year that @BSidesCLT hosted a conference beginning to end of it's own, to be held on May 6-7 at the beautiful UNCC Center City location.

Mission

Our mission is to serve the information security community in and around Charlotte, NC by offering low-cost educational opportunities for students of information security. Likewise, we seek to give professionals and experts the opportunity to share their love and craft with those interested students. We do this through our annual conference held in Charlotte, NC. We give preference to local folks who are willing to share what they have learned with others.
