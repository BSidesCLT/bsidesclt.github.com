BSides Charlotte

Welcome to BSides Charlotte. This site is the main location for information on the second annual BSides event in Charlotte, NC to be held June 21st. We are again partnering with SELF to once again bring timely, interesting, and relevant Information Security talks to a broader audience than what you normally find at a security conference. SELF is an excellent annual event put on by some hardworking people. By joining forces we’re lowering our expenses and able to focus on bringing you a quality security event. We’re planning some great talks.

If you’re interested in attending check the location link for more information.

Are you interested in presenting? Click the CFP link to submit your idea.

If you’re interested in sponsoring we’re currently seeking sponsors. Find out more information on our Sponsor page.

Follow along with the latest news and happening here at BSidesCLT by following our blog and @bsidesclt on Twitter.
