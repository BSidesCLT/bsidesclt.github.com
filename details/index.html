Parking

Friday May 6th, 2016:

Attendees can park and pay on their own at surface lots surrounding UNC Charlotte Center City, at metered spaces along Brevard and 8th Streets or in the deck at 7th Street Station. These are all within walking distance of Center City. Rates begin at $4 per car per day. Availability fluctuates depending on activity at Center City and the surrounding Uptown area, including events at nearby Time Warner Arena.

Saturday May 7th, 2016:

There is no charge for parking at surface lots surrounding UNC Charlotte Center City. There are metered spaces along Brevard and 8th Streets or in the deck at 7th Street Station.
